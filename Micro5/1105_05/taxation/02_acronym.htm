Acronyms

The business world is full of acronyms.

An acronym is an abbreviation created by using the initial letters of words or syllables and joining them together. Examples of acronyms are UFO (Unidentified Flying Object) or QANTAS (Queensland And Northern Territory Air Service).

Understanding taxation acronyms will make it easier for you to read and understand information on taxation or formal documentation sent from the taxation office.

Taxation acronyms (solo)
