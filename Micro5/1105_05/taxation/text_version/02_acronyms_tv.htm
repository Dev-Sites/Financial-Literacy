Taxation acronyms

When you read a taxation document it is highly likely it will contain some or all of the following acronyms. But do you know exactly what each of the acronyms represent?

Check your knowledge by writing the meaning of each of the acronyms in the following list. If you need assistance try the web site of the Australian Taxation Office web site www.ato.gov.au or alternatively use ATO publications and/or other web sites.

The answers are revealed when you scroll further down the page.

Acronyms:

TFN

GST

CGT

PAYG

ABN

BAS

FBT

 

 

 

 

 

Answers:

TFN = Tax File Number

GST = Goods and Services Tax

CGT = Capital Gains Tax

PAYG = Pay As You Go

ABN = Australian Business Number

BAS = Business Activity Statement

FBT = Fringe Benefits Tax
