Keeping records

Sales

Examples of sales records include:

  • sales invoices (including tax invoices)
  • sales receipts
  • cash register tapes
  • records of cash sales.

 

Expenses

Examples of purchase/expense records include:

  • purchase/expense invoices (including tax invoices)
  • purchase/expense receipts (which include an ABN)
  • cheque butts and bank account statements
  • credit card statements
  • diary showing petty cash purchases.

 

Year-end tax

Examples of year-end income income tax records include:

  • motor vehicle expenses
  • debtors and creditors lists
  • stock take sheets
  • depreciation schedules
  • capital gains tax records.

 

GST

Examples of GST records include:

  • tax invoices
  • adjustment notes
  • documents that show election, choice, estimate, determination or calculation.

 

Employees

Example of records relating to payments to employees include:

  • tax file number declarations and withholding declarations
  • withholding variation notices
  • worker payment records
  • PAYG payment summaries
  • annual reports
  • super records
  • records of any fringe benefits provided.

 

PAYG

Example of PAYG withholding records include:

  • records of amounts withheld from payments where no ABN was quoted
  • a copy of any PAYG withholding voluntary agreements
  • records of voluntary agreement payments
  • PAYG payment summaries
  • PAYG payment summary – employment termination payment summaries and ETP payment summaries
  • annual reports.

 

Fuel

Example of records relating to fuel tax credits include:

  • records of fuel acquired
  • records of eligible and ineligible fuel use
  • records of claim calculations
  • records of any loss, sale or disposal of fuel.
