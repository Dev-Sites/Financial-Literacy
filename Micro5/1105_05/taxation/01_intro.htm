Introduction

As a micro business owner there are a considerable number of taxation obligations to comply with. Obligations include completing taxation registrations, withholding amounts of money, lodging activity statements and taxation returns, and keeping necessary records.

Due to the knowledge and time required to meet these obligations, many businesses seek professional assistance. People who can assist in this area include bookkeeping professionals, taxation advisors and/or registered taxation agents.

Choose a link from the side menu to further your understanding on taxation requirements including:

  • acronyms
  • taxation terms
  • taxation registrations
  • record keeping.

As you move through the links, you will gather lots of useful information about insurance requirements. To help you organise this information, we suggest you store it in a Comply with regulatory, taxation and insurance requirements folder. Your folder could be:

  • paper-based, for example, manila folder, ring binder
  • electronic, for example, on your computer hard drive.

The information you collect will help you to complete the 'You do it' activity and could also be a useful reference in the future.
