You do it

Can you correctly identify, develop and implement compliance procedures to meet the necessary taxation requirements for your business?

Download and save a copy of the Taxation worksheet (Word 66kb).

When you have completed the worksheet you will have:

  • demonstrated an understanding of a range of taxation terms
  • determined taxation registrations for a business
  • determined record keeping requirements for a business.

If you are enrolled in a course with a registered training organisation, check with your trainer about completing this activity as part of the assessment requirements for the national unit of competency BSBSMB305A: Comply with regulatory, taxation and insurance requirements for the micro business.

You don't have to do this alone. Remember to refer to your Comply with regulatory, taxation and insurance requirements folder. There's also helpful information in the Business Support section.
