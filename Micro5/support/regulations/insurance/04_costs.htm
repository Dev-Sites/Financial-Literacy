The cost of insurance

Insurance is a necessary component of risk management, but it does come at a financial cost.

Following are some hints on minimising your micro business insurance costs.

  • Buy packaged policies instead of single policies. Packaged policies are tailor made for certain types of businesses, for example - Insurance packages for shops, offices, factories, trade contractors.
  • Consolidate all your insurance policies with one company. This can get you a discount with the company and they may be more inclined to honour claims.
  • Know how low you can go before you expose yourself to too much risk but do not under-insure.
  • Get a minimum of three quotes. Carefully check the clauses, to compare what you are covered for and the exclusions.
  • Review your cover annually. Circumstances and needs change, policies need to be kept up to date.
  • Use an insurance broker. National Insurance Brokers Association of Australia (NIBA)
