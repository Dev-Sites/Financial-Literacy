Working from home

Many people wrongly assume that domestic insurance will provide cover while they are working from home.
The insurance requirements for a home business are exactly the same as those for a business located in commercial premises.

Home insurance does not cover:

  • Professional indemnity
  • Business interruption
  • Public and products liability
  • Workcover
  • Property damage of work related contents such as business stock, materials and equipment
  • Transit costs
  • Personal accident or illness

Failure to notify your insurance company about your home based business may cause loss of insurance cover on the house even if it is destroyed by a non-business event.
