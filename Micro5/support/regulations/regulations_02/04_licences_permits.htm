Licences and permits

There are permit and licencing requirements for a range of business related matters and professions, including:

 
Zoning Advertising Agent
Parking requirements Air services
Sign writing Appraiser or assessor
Access Auctioneer
Landscaping Busker
Alterations/extensions Broker or agent
Health regulations Real Estate Agent
Trading hours Electrician
Industrial awards Second hand dealer
Gas, electricity and water Stockbroker
Environment Firearms dealer
Hazardous chemicals Chemist
Fire regulations Importer
Discharge of waste Tax agent
Weights and measures Liquor seller

 

Federal, state and territory and local governments are all responsible for the different licences, permits, registrations and certificates that apply to businesses.

For licencing details access the following web sites:

Australian Capital Territory - www.blis.act.gov.au

New South Wales - http://blis.fairtrading.nsw.gov.au

Northern Territory - www.bli.net.au/nt

South Australia - http://sa.bli.net.au

Queensland - www.sd.qld.gov.au

Tasmania - www.blis.tas.gov.au

Victoria - http://blis.business.vic.gov.au

West Australia - www.licence.sbdc.com.au
