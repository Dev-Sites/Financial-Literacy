Other useful websites

National Insurance Brokers Association
http://www.niba.com.au

Business Enterprise Centres (BECs) throughout all states and territories in Australia work with and on behalf of the micro and small business sectors. To find your closest BEC, visit the link and search under 'Find a BEC'.
http://www.beca.org.au

The Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD) website has information about a range of issues including economy, trade, development, environment, energy and social.
http:www.oecd.org

The Small Business Support Network provides ideas, advice and ongoing support to small business operators.
http://www.ozsmallbiz.net

The New Enterprise Incentive Scheme (NEIS) helps eligible unemployed people to start up and run a new, viable small business.
http://www.workplace.gov.au

- Programs
- New Enterprise Incentive Schems (NEIS).

Young Achievement Australia is a not-for-profit organisation that offers business programs to young Australians.
http://www.yaa.org.au/

 

Commercial websites

A number of commercial websites can provide you with information on how to run your small business. You may need to be a member to use all the resources and there may be charges for some services.

Catapult is an ABC website for young innovators and small businesses.
http://www.abc.net.au
Type catapult in the ABC search engine.

Dynamic Business magazine provides advice and articles.
http://www.dynamicbusiness.com

Dynamic Export is a magazine for growing business overseas.
http://www.dynamicexport.com.au

Dynamic Small Business Network offers free tools such as checklists and planning templates.
http://www.dsbn.com.au

Entrepreneur Business Centre has lots of free downloads and other resources.
http://www.ebc.com.au

Micro and Home Based Business Network includes information about home based internet businesses as well as general small business information.
http://www.mbn.com.au

Both the NineMSN and the Yahoo small business websites have lots of information ranging from planning to running a small business.

NineMSN small business website:
http://ninemsn.com.au
From the menu select:
- Money
- Small business.

Yahoo small business website
http://au.smallbusiness.yahoo.com

Flying solo is an online community for solo and micro business owners.
http://www.flyingsolo.com.au

Business Innovation & Incubation Australia is an association of business incubators and people interested in business incubation. Business incubators are non-profit development centres that provide a range of business services, for start-up businesses, in a nurturing and supportive environment.
http://www.businessincubation.com.au
