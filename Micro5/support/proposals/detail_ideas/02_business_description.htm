Business description

A business idea may be supported by many different stakeholders (some more important than others due to their level of commitment to the business idea).

Each stakeholder needs to be informed of the business idea in a way that suits their purposes, for example, a bank or lender needs to be clearly informed of the business idea to assist in mitigating their risk.

The more accurate the business description is, the less likelihood there is to be any surprises, for example, disputes surrounding the nature of the business idea.
