Business summary

An accurate summary of the major products or services of the business is needed so that all stakeholders have an understanding of the business and what it does in the market place. The products or services the business sells can be detailed with respect to personal needs and requirements.

Personal needs are typically emotions or feelings that a customer is looking to have satisfied from purchasing a specific product or service. It is said that the prime motivation of a customer is to have a need (sometime more generally referred to as a want) fulfilled. Once this need can be seen as being met by a specific product or service the customer will typically be interested in features or specifics of the product or service.

The summary of products or services should be done along the lines of major different classifications in style of product(s) or service(s). For example, if a business was in the motor car industry then major classifications of product could be: small 2-door vehicles; small 4-door vehicles; medium sized vehicles; large sized vehicles; sports motor vehicles; commercial motor vehicles.
