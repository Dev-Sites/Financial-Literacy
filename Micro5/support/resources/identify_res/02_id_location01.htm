Location... location... location

Finding the right location for a business premises is important. Different factors affect different businesses, so use the questions to help you work out the factors that are important for your business.

Customers

If customers need to come to your place of business:

  • Is car parking available close by?
  • Is it close to public transport?
  • Is it consistent with your image?
  • Is it what customers want and expect?
  • Where is the competition located?

Employees

If employees need to come to your place of business:

  • What's the likely availability of qualified people in the immediate area?
  • Is there adequate car parking close by?
  • Is it close to public transport?

Suppliers

If you will need to visit suppliers, or have supplies delivered to your place of business, close proximity means faster delivery and lower costs:

  • How far away are your main suppliers?
