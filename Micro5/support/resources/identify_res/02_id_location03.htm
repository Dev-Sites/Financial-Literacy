Virtual business

The use of the internet in the running of businesses is growing. Some businesses don't even maintain a store front, and trade entirely over the web. This can save time and money and basically make the whole world the marketplace.

To run a business online, you need a website with a unique web address. A web address looks something like: www.mybusinessname.com.au

A simple website might include information about a business, range of products and/or services and contact details. More sophisticated websites could allow customers to check availability of stock, place an order and pay online.

Remember that you will also need to find out what registration and licensing obligations apply for your business in your state or territory.

Check business.gov.au (opens new window) and look for more information by selecting e-business.
