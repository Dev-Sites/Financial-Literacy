Acquiring resources

Some options for acquiring resources, together with some points for and against each one, are provided below.

You should always seek professional advice before entering into any rental, hire or leasing agreement.

Acquiring resources
Option For Against
Leasing
  • No need for large lump sum.
  • Payments may be tax deductible.
  • You don't own the equipment.
  • Must make regular payments over a period of time.
  • If you don't pay the agreed amount on time the items may be taken away by the lessor.
Hire purchase
  • No need for large lump sum.
  • You will own the asset at the end of the payment period.
  • Interest and depreciation may be tax deductible.
  • Must make regular payments over a period of time.
  • Pay interest on top of the cost of the item.
Purchase outright
  • You own the asset.
  • No additional interest to pay.
  • No ongoing repayments.
  • Potential for supplier discount.
  • Depreciation may be tax deductible.
  • Requires ready cash lump sum.
  • Could lead to cash flow problems.
