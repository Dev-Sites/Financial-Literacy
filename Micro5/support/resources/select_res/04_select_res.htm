Reviewing suppliers

It's advisable to review the service and costs of your suppliers on a regular basis, for example, once a year or after a contract. Look at ways you could improve your business with the supplier. Here are some points to consider.

  • Could costs be reduced by negotiating a better deal?
  • Would changing the delivery schedule or transport mode lower your costs?
  • How could you improve communication with your suppliers?
  • Have there been any problems with the supplier's product over the past year?
  • Would it be more cost effective to buy in larger quantities?
  • Would it be more cost effective to buy from an off shore / on shore supplier?
  • Are there hidden costs in buying from another supplier?
