Commercial websites

A number of commercial websites can provide you with information on how to run your small business. You may need to be a member to use all the resources and there may be charges for some services.

Micro and Home Based Business Network includes information about home based internet businesses as well as general small business information.
http://www.mbn.com.au

Both the NineMSN and the Yahoo small business websites have lots of information about business resources.

NineMSN small business website:
http://ninemsn.com.au/
From the menu select:
- Money
- Small business.

Yahoo small business website
http://au.smallbusiness.yahoo.com

Business Innovation & Incubation Australia is an association of business incubators and people interested in business incubation.
http://www.businessincubation.com.au

More about setting up a home business
http://www.business.gov.au
From the menu select:
- Home-based business
