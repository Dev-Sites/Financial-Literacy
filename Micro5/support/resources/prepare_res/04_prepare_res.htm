Replacing equipment

Equipment is replaced for a variety of reasons, including:

  • increase in frequency of breakdowns
  • updating technology and systems
  • production process modification
  • comply with occupational health and safety requirements.

It is very important to thoroughly research new equipment. Some areas to consider are:

  • size and weight
  • power consumption
  • cost
  • efficiency
  • servicing arrangements
  • availability of spare parts.
