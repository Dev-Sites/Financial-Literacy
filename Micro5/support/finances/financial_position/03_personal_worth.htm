Personal worth

This involves putting an accurate dollar value on what you own and what you owe.

What you own could include:

  • house
  • car
  • investments
  • boat
  • rental property
  • cash in bank.
 

What you owe could include:

  • mortgage
  • personal loan
  • credit card debt
  • motor vehicle loan.
 

Your personal worth is the total value of what you own, less the total value of what you owe.
