Your finances

A good way to begin working out whether to start your own business is to look at your personal/family financial situation.

Start by calculating your disposable income.

Step one

List your income streams. There's an example in the table.

 
Income received Amount Frequency
Part time job $400 Fortnight
Rent received $1,000 Month

 

Step two

List your financial commitments and money required for living expenses. There's an example in the table.

 
Outgoings Amount Frequency
Mortgage $400 Month
Telephone $100 Month
Food $300 Week
School Fees $500 Term
Clothing $100 Quarter
Health Insurance $250 Month

 

Step three

Work out your disposable income by using this equation:

Disposable income = income - outgoings.

Note: Take into account the frequency of the income and payments when you are calculating disposable income.
