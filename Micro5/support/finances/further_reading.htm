Other useful websites

Business Enterprise Centres (BECs) throughout all states and territories in Australia work with and on behalf of the and small business sectors. To find your closest BEC, visit the link and search under 'Find a BEC'.
http://www.beca.org.au

The New Enterprise Incentive Scheme (NEIS) helps eligible unemployed people to start up and run a new, viable small business.
http://www.workplace.gov.au
From the menu select:
- Programs
- New Enterprise Incentive Scheme (NEIS).

AusIndustry is responsible for delivering innovation grants, tax and duty concessoins, small business development, industry support and venture capital.
http://www.ausindustry.gov.au

The Australian Taxation Office provides specific information related to taxation issues.
http://www.ato.gov.au
