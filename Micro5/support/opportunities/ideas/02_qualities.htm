Personal qualities

Because the owner/manager of a micro business is the main driving force behind the business, as well as the major decision maker, that person's personal characteristics and skills are crucial to the survival of the business.

Research attempting to identify the typical successful business operator suggests the characteristics listed in our A to Z list are found frequently among successful managers.

  • Aspiration | Action-orientation | Assertiveness | Ability to listen to advice
  • Competitive spirit
  • Decision-making skills | Desire to excel
  • Enjoyment of challenges | Entrepreneurial attitude | Enthusiasm
  • Forbearance          
  • Goal-setting skills
  • Innovativeness | Internal driving force
  • Leadership qualities | Long-term commitment
  • Money as a motive | Motivation
  • Planning ability | Positive attitude | Problem-solving skills
  • Realistic optimism | Resolve | Resourcefulness
  • Self-assurance | Self-determination | Self-discipline
  • Task-orientation | Tenacity            
  • Work-orientation
  • Zeal
