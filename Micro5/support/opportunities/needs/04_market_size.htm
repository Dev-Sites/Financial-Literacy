Market size

Why collect market size information?

It is important to collect market size information to work out whether the market is large enough to justify further investigation and exploration. 

Knowing the market size is necessary for a range of reasons.

  • If the market too small, it may be too risky to pursue a potential business. Perhaps the market is already well serviced by existing market players. 
  • The market may be sufficient in size, but declining at a rate that makes it too risky for a new market entrant.

If the size of the market appears to be satisfactory, then its potential for a new business entrant must be measured. This is because all potential and existing market players are different from each other. What is seen as a great opportunity for one player could be seen as too risky for another. For example, some potential market entrants may already be operating in markets with similar needs, while other entrants may be new to the market and/or micro business and may be more aware of risk.

Appropriate information about market size and potential can be gathered from secondary sources as well as expert interviews or opinions.
