Primary research example

This example looks at Ted, a small business owner, who is keen to target budget-minded consumers who want to buy a dining table and chairs.

Ted has decided to develop his own survey tool to gain the information he needs from the part of the market he wishes to target. Ted has decided that the survey needs to take in to account the following issues:

  • behaviour of the market, for example, what they have, what they want, when they want it, where they shop, how they like to shop, etc
  • what the target market likes and dislikes about better and poorer retailers
  • any unmet needs
  • pricing feedback
  • promotional feedback
  • business name options.

Here's what Ted might say to a prospective consumer.

Introduction

Hello I'm Ted. I'm interested in your thoughts on dining furniture.  Would you have five minutes to assist me with some questions?

Questions

  1. How many tables with at least four chairs do you have in your home?
  2. How often do you have friends and family over for dinners and/or lunches at your home?
  3. When do you think you might be in the market for a new or replacement dining setting?
  4. What style (or styles) of dining setting do you like?
  5. What colour scheme do you prefer for your new dining setting?
  6. How many chairs will you require in your new dining setting?
  7. Do you have a preferred supplier or retailer for dining setting furniture? If so, who is your preferred supplier or retailer?
  8. What did you most like about a positive retail furniture experience (whether you were purchasing or browsing) you've had in recent years?
  9. What do you most dislike about a negative retail furniture experiences (whether you were purchasing or browsing) you've had in recent years?
  10. Are there any dining furniture pieces or styles that you find difficult to locate?
  11. What is your budget range for a new dining setting?
  12. How do you locate a suitable brand (or retailer) of dining furniture in your budget range?
  13. Imagine that a new dining furniture brand is going to enter the market in the coming months. Which of the following three brand names do you prefer?
    • Brand A
    • Brand B
    • Brand C.
