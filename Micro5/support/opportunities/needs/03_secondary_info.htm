Secondary research example

This is an example of secondary market research into the Australian furniture industry. This information has been gathered from a published source, which is accessible to the public. The information used has been sourced from IBISWORLD, a business futurology forecasting service for 496 industries within Australia.

The following information is a statistical analysis of the furniture industry over the last three years and a future projection for this industry over the next three years.

Retail component of the industry - Australia

 
Year 2004-05 2005-06 2006-07
Retail size $million $5919.0 $6143.5 $6372.0
% change on prior year +2.0% +3.8% +3.7%
Number of furniture retailers in Australia 4,332 4,484 4,613

Comments

The retail value growth of the total furniture market in Australia is healthy and in line with general economic performance over the same time frame (ie GDP growth has been around 3% to 5% in Australia over this period).

There is good growth in the number of retailers within the furniture sector in Australia. It is continually trending upwards, with 2.8% growth for the last time period.

Major retail players in Australia

The leading four retailers in domestic furniture in Australia are:

  • Steinhoff Asia Pacific Holdings Pty Ltd
  • Forty Winks Pty Ltd
  • Harvey Norman Holdings Pty Ltd
  • Super A Mart Pty Ltd.
